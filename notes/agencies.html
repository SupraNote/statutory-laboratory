Department of Administrative Services

Headed by Director of Administrative Services. Neb. Rev. Stat. § 81-1103. Appointed by Governor. Id. Serves at the discretion of the Governor. Id.

Statutorily-created Divisions:

  1. Accounting Division. Neb. Rev. Stat. § 81-1108.
    1. Headed by Administrator, Accounting Division. Id.
    2. Appointed by Director of Administrative Services. Id.
    3. Serves at pleasure of Director of Administrative Services. Id.
  2. Budget Division. Id.
    1. Headed by Administrator, Budget Division. Id.
    2. Appointed by Director of Administrative Services. Id.
    3. Serves at pleasure of Director of Administrative Services. Id.
  3. Information Management Services Division. Id.
    1. Headed by Administrator, Information Management Services Division. Id.; see also id. § 81-1116.
    2. Appointed by Chief Information Officer. Id. § 81-1116.
  4. Materiel Division. Id. § 81-1108.
    1. Headed by Materiel Adminstrator. Id. § 81-152.
    2. Appointed by Director of Administrative Services. Id. § 81-147.
    3. Supervised by Office of the Director of Administrative Services. Id. § 81-14591).
    4. Must take Oath of Office. Id. § 81-150.
    5. Consists of the following branches:
      1. Office Supplies Bureau. Id. § 81-152(1).
      2. Central mail. Id. § 81-152(2).
      3. Print Shop. Id. § 81-152(3).
      4. Copy Services. Id. § 81-152(4).
      5. State Purchasing Bureau. Id. § 81-152(5).
      6. State Recycling Office. Id. § 81-152(6).
      7. State Surplus Property. Id. § 81-152(7).
  5. State Building Division. Id. § 81-1108.
    1. Headedd by Administrator, State Building Division. Id.
    2. Appointed by Director of Administrative Services. Id.
  6. Office of Chief Information Officer.
    1. Headed by Chief Information Officer.
    2. Has the following divisions:
      1. Division of Communications. Id. §§  81-1108, 81-1120.03.
        1. Headed by Director of Communnications. Id. § 81-1120.03.
        2. Appointed by Chief Information Officer. Id.
  7. Personnel Division. Id. § 81-1108.
    1. Headed by Director of Personnel. Id.
    2. Appointed by Director of Administrative Services. Id.
  8. Division of Employee Relations Id. 81-1108.
    1. Headed by Chief Negotiator. Id. § 81-1376. Director of Administrative Services may serve as the Chief Negotiator. Id.
    2. Appointed by Governor. Id.
    3. Serves at pleasure of Governor. Id.
    4. Represents Governor. Id.
  9. Risk Managment and State Claims Division. Id. 81-1108.
    1. Headed by Risk Manager. Id. § 81-8,239.01(2).
    2. Appointed by Director of Administrative Services. Id.
    3. Reesponsible for the Risk Management Program. Id.
  10. Transportation Services Bureau. Id. § 81-1108.
    1. Headed by Chief, Transportation Services Bureau. Id. § 81-1009.
    2. Appointed by Director of Administrative Services. Id.
